Lead Zinc Titanates are known for their high electromechanical coupling coefficients, making them ideal for sensors and actuators. Lead Titanate is renowned for its strong piezoelectric properties and is often used in high-frequency applications. Lead Magnesium Niobate, with its exceptional thermal stability and acoustic properties, is increasingly favored in advanced transducer technologies. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries.</p></p>
