<p><p>The FMCG print label market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Wet glued labels are applied using adhesives and are common in food products. Pressure sensitive or self-adhesive labels stick easily to surfaces without additional glue. Liner-less labels eliminate the backing material, reducing waste. Multi-part barcode labels facilitate tracking and inventory management. In-mold labels are integrated during product molding for durability, while shrink sleeve labels conform to container shapes, providing a 360-degree branding opportunity.</p></p>
