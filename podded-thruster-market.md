<p><ul><li>Built-in Motor</li><li>External Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The podded thruster market can be categorized into two main types based on motor configuration: built-in motor and external motor. Built-in motor thrusters feature integrated propulsion systems within the pod, optimizing space and reducing maintenance needs. In contrast, external motor thrusters separate the motor from the pod, allowing for greater flexibility in installation and potential upgrades. Both types serve essential roles in maritime applications, enhancing maneuverability and efficiency for vessels while catering to varying operational requirements and design preferences.</p></p>
