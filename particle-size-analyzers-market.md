<p><ul><li>Nano Particle Size Analyzers</li><li>Micron Particle Size Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The particle size analyzers market is primarily divided into two segments: nano particle size analyzers and micron particle size analyzers. Nano particle size analyzers are designed to measure extremely small particles, typically below 100 nanometers, using techniques such as dynamic light scattering and electron microscopy. Micron particle size analyzers, on the other hand, focus on larger particles, generally ranging from 1 micron to several hundred microns, employing methods like laser diffraction and sieve analysis. Both types are essential for applications in pharmaceuticals, materials science, and food industries.</p></p>
