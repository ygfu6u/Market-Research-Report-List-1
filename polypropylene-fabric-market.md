<p><ul><li>Homopolymer Polypropylene</li><li>Copolymer Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene fabric market is primarily categorized into two types: homopolymer polypropylene and copolymer polypropylene. Homopolymer polypropylene, known for its rigidity and high strength, is often used in applications requiring durability, such as automotive components and packaging. In contrast, copolymer polypropylene offers greater flexibility and impact resistance, making it suitable for products like textiles and containers. Both types cater to diverse industries, with specific properties enhancing their performance across various applications, from manufacturing to everyday consumer goods.</p></p>
