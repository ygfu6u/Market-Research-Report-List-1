<p><p>The Wavelength Division Multiplexer (WDM) market is primarily divided into two types: Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexer (CWDM) and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexer (DWDM). CWDM utilizes a broader wavelength range, allowing fewer channels per fiber, making it ideal for short-distance applications and cost-sensitive setups. In contrast, DWDM offers a higher channel density with tightly spaced wavelengths, enabling extensive data transmission over long distances, which is vital for high-capacity networks and telecommunications, supporting the growing demand for bandwidth.</p></p>
