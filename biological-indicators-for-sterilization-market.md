<p><ul><li>Self-Contained Biological Indicator</li><li>Biological Indicator Strip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterilization market features two primary types of biological indicators: self-contained biological indicators and biological indicator strips. Self-contained biological indicators are ready-to-use devices that include spores and growth media in a single unit, enabling straightforward monitoring of sterilization processes. Biological indicator strips, on the other hand, consist of strips impregnated with spores that require separate incubation after exposure to sterilization to assess efficacy. Both types play essential roles in verifying the effectiveness of sterilization practices in healthcare and laboratory settings.</p></p>
