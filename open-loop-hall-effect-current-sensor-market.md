<p><p>The Open-Loop Hall-Effect Current Sensor market is categorized into two main types: Linear Output and Threshold Output. </p><p>Linear Output sensors provide a continuous voltage or current signal proportional to the detected current, enabling precise monitoring and control in various applications. </p><p>Threshold Output sensors, on the other hand, activate a binary signal when a specific current level is detected, ideal for applications requiring simple on/off control based on current thresholds, such as overcurrent protection systems. Both types cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
