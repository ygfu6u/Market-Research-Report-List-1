<p><p>The auto storage battery market encompasses various types, including Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd), Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH), and Lithium-Ion (Li-ion) batteries. NiCd batteries are known for durability and performance in extreme conditions but suffer from memory effect. NiMH batteries offer higher energy density and are commonly used in hybrid vehicles. Lithium-Ion batteries dominate the market due to their lightweight, high energy capacity, and long cycle life, making them ideal for electric vehicles. Each type serves distinct automotive needs and applications.</p></p>
