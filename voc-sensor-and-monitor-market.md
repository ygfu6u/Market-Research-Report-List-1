<p><strong>The VOC Sensor and Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochemical Type</li><li>PID Type</li><li>Metal oxide (MOS) Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC sensor and monitor market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Electrochemical sensors detect volatile organic compounds by measuring changes in electric current, offering high sensitivity. PID (Photoionization Detector) type sensors utilize UV light to ionize gases, providing rapid and accurate detection of VOCs. Metal oxide semiconductor (MOS) sensors operate by measuring resistance changes in the presence of VOCs, ideal for broader air quality monitoring. Other types may include infrared sensors or photoacoustic devices, broadening market diversity.</p></p>
