<p><p>The automotive environmental tire market is primarily segmented into all-steel and semi-steel tires. Semi-steel tires, on the other hand, utilize a combination of steel and textile materials, offering a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness. They are lighter and more flexible, making them suitable for passenger vehicles, contributing to improved fuel efficiency and ride comfort.</p></p>
