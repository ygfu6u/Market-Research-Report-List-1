<p><ul><li>Spot Welding Robot</li><li>Arc Welding Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 6-axis welding robot market is primarily divided into two types: spot welding robots and arc welding robots. Spot welding robots are designed for quick, localized welding tasks, commonly used in automotive manufacturing for joining sheet metal components. In contrast, arc welding robots perform continuous welding along seams, suited for applications requiring stronger, more intricate welds, like in construction and heavy machinery. Both types enhance efficiency, precision, and productivity in various industrial sectors, adapting to specific welding needs.</p></p>
