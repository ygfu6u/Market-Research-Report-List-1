<p><strong>The Blood Irradiator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Blood Irradiation</li><li>Gamma-ray Blood Irradiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood irradiator market is divided into two primary types: X-ray blood irradiation and gamma-ray blood irradiation. X-ray blood irradiation utilizes high-energy X-rays to sterilize blood products, effectively eliminating pathogens while preserving the integrity of the blood components. In contrast, gamma-ray blood irradiation employs gamma radiation from isotopes like Cobalt-60 or Cesium-137 for the same purpose, providing deep penetration and efficient disinfection. Both methods are essential for ensuring the safety of blood transfusions and enhancing patient care in healthcare settings.</p></p>
