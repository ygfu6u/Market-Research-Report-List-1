<p><p>The Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD) equipment market is categorized into two main types: plate type and tubular type. Plate-type PECVD systems utilize flat plates to generate plasma, allowing for uniform deposition across large substrates, making them ideal for semiconductor and solar cell applications. In contrast, tubular-type PECVD systems feature cylindrical reactors that provide efficient gas flow and plasma generation, suitable for coating complex geometries, such as fibers and small parts. Both types serve distinct industrial needs in advanced material processing.</p></p>
