<p><p>Juice puree and concentrate are processed forms of fruit and vegetable juices, primarily used in the food and beverage industry. These products offer convenience and longer shelf life, making them popular choices for manufacturers.</p><p>The Juice Puree and Concentrate Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.90% during the forecast period.
