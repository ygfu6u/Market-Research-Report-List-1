<p><p>The automotive parking assistance system market primarily comprises two key types: cameras and ultrasonic sensors. Camera-based systems utilize visual imaging to provide real-time video feeds, enhancing spatial awareness for drivers during parking maneuvers. They often include features like rearview or 360-degree views. Ultrasonic sensors, on the other hand, use sound waves to detect obstacles, providing auditory or visual alerts based on proximity. Both technologies enhance safety and convenience, catering to consumer demand for improved parking capabilities in modern vehicles.</p></p>
