<p><ul><li>Wet Ground</li><li>Dry Ground</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mica powder market is primarily categorized into two types: wet ground and dry ground mica. Wet ground mica is produced through a process that uses water to grind mica flakes into a fine powder, resulting in a smoother texture, making it ideal for applications like cosmetics and paints. In contrast, dry ground mica is created through a dry milling process, resulting in coarser particles suitable for industries such as plastics and construction. Each type caters to specific industrial needs based on texture and application.</p></p>
