<p><p>A pallet is a flat transport structure used to support goods in a stable manner while being lifted by forklifts, pallet jacks, or other handling equipment. Pallets are typically made from wood, plastic, metal, or composite materials, with wood being the most widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and durability.</p><p>The Pallet Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for efficient logistics and supply chain solutions.
