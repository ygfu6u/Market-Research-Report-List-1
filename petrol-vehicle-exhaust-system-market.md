<p><p>The petrol vehicle exhaust system market includes two main types: single and dual exhaust systems. A single exhaust system uses one pipe to expel gases, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity, suitable for standard vehicles. A dual exhaust system employs two pipes, enhancing engine performance, fuel efficiency, and sound by reducing backpressure. While single systems are common in everyday cars, dual systems are preferred in high-performance or luxury vehicles for improved power and aesthetics. Both systems aim to optimize emissions and vehicle performance.</p></p>
