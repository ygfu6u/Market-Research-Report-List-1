<p><p>The Serration Balloon Catheter market is categorized into two primary types: Over-the-Wire (OTW) Serration Balloon Catheters and Rapid Exchange Serration Balloon Catheters. OTW Serration Balloon Catheters utilize a guidewire for navigation and can facilitate procedures requiring precise placement. In contrast, Rapid Exchange Serration Balloon Catheters enable quick exchange of devices with a shorter length of guidewire, allowing for faster access and improved maneuverability in complex vascular interventions. Both types are critical in enhancing patient outcomes in therapeutic procedures.</p></p>
