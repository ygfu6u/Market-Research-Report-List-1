<p><p>The Tour Guide System market is divided into two main types: Wire Tour Guide Systems and Wireless Tour Guide Systems. Wire Tour Guide Systems involve physical connections, often using microphones and headphones, providing reliable sound transmission but limiting mobility. In contrast, Wireless Tour Guide Systems use advanced technology such as RF or infrared communication, allowing for greater freedom of movement and convenience for both guides and participants. This flexibility enhances the overall touring experience by allowing dynamic interaction and exploration.</p></p>
