<p><p>Visitor Management Software (VMS) can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise VMS is installed locally on a company's servers, allowing for greater control and customization, but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based VMS operates on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from anywhere with an internet connection.
