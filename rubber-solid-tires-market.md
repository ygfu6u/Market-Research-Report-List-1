<p><p>The rubber solid tires market is primarily segmented into bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers of fabric running diagonally, providing flexibility and a sturdy sidewall, making them suitable for off-road applications. In contrast, radial tires have fabric layers arranged radially, allowing for better traction, improved fuel efficiency, and longer lifespan, commonly used in vehicles requiring high performance. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in industrial, agricultural, and automotive sectors, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
