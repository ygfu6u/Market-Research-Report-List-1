<p><p>Cleanroom laminar flow units are essential for maintaining sterile environments in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and electronics. They are categorized into two market types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical laminar flow units direct airflow from the ceiling downwards, effectively minimizing contamination by creating a protective air curtain. In contrast, horizontal units direct airflow from one side to another, offering a larger working space but may be less effective in certain applications. Both types ensure a controlled, particle-free environment for sensitive processes.</p></p>
