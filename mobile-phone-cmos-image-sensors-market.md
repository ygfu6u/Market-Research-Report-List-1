<p><strong>The Mobile Phone CMOS Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stacked CMOS</li><li>Back-illuminated CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile phone CMOS image sensors are crucial for capturing high-quality images and video. The stacked CMOS market involves integrating multiple layers, enhancing pixel density and reducing space, which improves performance and features like autofocus and HDR. In contrast, back-illuminated CMOS sensors position light-sensitive areas in a way that maximizes light capture, resulting in better low-light performance and image quality. Both types cater to the growing demand for advanced camera capabilities in smartphones, influencing design and user experience.</p></p>
