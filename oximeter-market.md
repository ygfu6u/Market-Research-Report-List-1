<p><p>The oximeter market comprises various types, primarily including finger oximeters, handheld oximeters, and others. Finger oximeters are compact, portable devices that clamp onto a fingertip to quickly measure blood oxygen levels and pulse rate, widely used for home monitoring. Handheld oximeters are larger, often more sophisticated devices suitable for clinical settings, providing accurate readings in real-time.
