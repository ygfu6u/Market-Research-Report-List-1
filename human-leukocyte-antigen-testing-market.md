<p><p>Human Leukocyte Antigen (HLA) testing is crucial for organ transplantation, disease diagnosis, and research. The market for HLA testing is categorized into two main types: molecular assay technologies and non-molecular assay technologies. Molecular assays include techniques like polymerase chain reaction (PCR) and sequencing, offering high sensitivity and specificity for HLA typing. In contrast, non-molecular assays encompass methods such as microcytotoxicity and serology, which, while less precise, remain cost-effective and are often used for initial screenings. Both technologies play vital roles in personalized medicine and compatibility assessments.</p></p>
