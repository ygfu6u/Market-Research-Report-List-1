<p><ul><li>Horizontal Ground Source Heat Pump</li><li>Vertical Ground Source Heat Pump</li><li>Surface Water Ground Source Heat Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Open-Loop Ground Source Heat Pump market comprises three primary types. Horizontal Ground Source Heat Pumps utilize shallow, extensive loops buried in the ground, ideal for spacious properties. Vertical Ground Source Heat Pumps, in contrast, rely on deep boreholes, making them suitable for limited spaces while maximizing heat exchange efficiency. Surface Water Ground Source Heat Pumps leverage nearby bodies of water, utilizing their thermal energy for heating and cooling. Each type offers distinct advantages based on site conditions and user requirements.</p></p>
