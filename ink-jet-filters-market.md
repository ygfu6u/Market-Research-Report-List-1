<p><p>The ink-jet filters market encompasses various ink types, including pigment-based, dye-based, and solvent-based inks. Pigment-based inks are known for their lightfastness and durability, making them ideal for high-quality prints. Dye-based inks offer vibrant color output but may lack longevity. Solvent-based inks are favored for their adhesion to non-porous surfaces and outdoor use. Each type of ink requires specific filter technologies to ensure optimal performance, as their unique properties necessitate tailored filtration solutions to maintain print quality and equipment longevity.</p></p>
