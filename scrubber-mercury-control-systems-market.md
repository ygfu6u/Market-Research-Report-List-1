<p><p>The Scrubber Mercury Control Systems market is divided into two primary types: dry control systems and wet control systems. Dry control systems utilize adsorption and filtration techniques to capture mercury from flue gases, offering lower water usage and operational simplicity. In contrast, wet control systems employ chemical reactions and scrubbing liquids to remove mercury, often achieving higher removal efficiencies. Each system type caters to different industrial needs and regulatory requirements, impacting their adoption in power plants and industrial facilities.</p></p>
