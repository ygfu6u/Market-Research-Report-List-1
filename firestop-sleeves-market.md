<p><p>The firestop sleeves market is segmented into pouring and embedded types. In contrast, embedded firestop sleeves are pre-installed within walls or floors, allowing for easier installation of pipes while providing a built-in fire-resistance solution. Both types are crucial for maintaining structural integrity and safety in building designs and renovations.</p></p>
