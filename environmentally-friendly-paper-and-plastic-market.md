<p><p>Environmentally friendly paper and plastic markets focus on sustainable production methods that minimize ecological impact. In dry pressing, materials are compacted into sheets using heat and pressure without water, resulting in lower energy consumption and waste. Wet pressing, on the other hand, involves using water to enhance fiber bonding during manufacturing, which can create stronger products but may use more resources. Both methods aim to reduce environmental footprints while meeting the demand for sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
