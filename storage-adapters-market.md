<p><p>The storage adapters market encompasses various types tailored for different networking protocols. Fibre Channel (FC) adapters offer high-speed, reliable connections for storage area networks, primarily in enterprise environments. Fibre Channel over Ethernet (FCoE) integrates FC's benefits into Ethernet networks, providing an efficient solution for data centers. iSCSI adapters utilize TCP/IP networks to link storage devices, enabling lower-cost deployments while maintaining performance. Each type serves unique needs, balancing speed, cost, and infrastructure compatibility in modern data management systems.</p></p>
