<p><p>The Natural Fatty Alcohols market is primarily segmented into two types: saturated and unsaturated alcohols. Saturated alcohols, derived from sources like palm oil and coconut oil, contain no double bonds in their carbon chains, resulting in higher stability and longer shelf life, commonly used in detergents and cosmetics. Unsaturated alcohols, featuring one or more double bonds, are more reactive and often employed in the production of surfactants and emulsifiers. Both types serve diverse applications in various industries, contributing to market growth.</p></p>
