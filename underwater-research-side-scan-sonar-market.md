<p><p>The underwater research side scan sonar market is divided into two primary types: single-beam and multi-beam sonar systems. Single-beam sonar focuses on a narrow strip of the seafloor, providing high-resolution images along a direct path but limited coverage. In contrast, multi-beam sonar emits multiple beams simultaneously, capturing wider areas with detailed topography, enhancing data collection efficiency. These technologies are crucial for marine surveys, archaeological explorations, and environmental monitoring, catering to diverse applications in underwater research.</p></p>
