<p><p>The Passenger Vehicle Smart Key market includes two main types: Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicle without physically using the key, as the system detects the key fob's proximity. RKES requires manual interaction, such as pressing a button on the key fob to lock, unlock, or start the vehicle. Both systems enhance convenience and security, with PKES offering a more seamless, hands-free experience compared to RKES.</p></p>
