<p><p>The uncooled infrared detector market comprises several types, each serving specific applications. Bolometers measure infrared radiation by detecting temperature changes in a material. Pyroelectric detectors sense variations in infrared light based on thermal energy changes. Thermopile detectors convert thermal energy into voltage and are suitable for temperature measurements. Diode detectors utilize semiconductor properties to detect infrared radiation. Thermal capacitance detectors measure changes in heat capacity. Other market segments may include hybrid detectors and specialized technologies tailored for niche applications.</p></p>
