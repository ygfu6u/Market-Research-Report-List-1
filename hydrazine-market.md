<p><p>The hydrazine market comprises three main types: Ultra-Pure Hydrazine, Anhydrous Hydrazine, and Hydrazine Hydrate. Anhydrous Hydrazine is a water-free form, primarily used in rocket propulsion and energy applications due to its efficient energy release. Hydrazine Hydrate contains water and is commonly utilized in agricultural, chemical synthesis, and polymer industries, serving varied industrial needs based on different purity and concentration requirements.</p></p>
