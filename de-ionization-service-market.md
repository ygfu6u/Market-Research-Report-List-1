<p><p>The De-Ionization Service Market primarily consists of two types: Dual-Beds and Mixed-Beds. Dual-Beds systems utilize two separate tanks, typically one for cation exchange and another for anion exchange, allowing for effective water purification. In contrast, Mixed-Beds combine both types of resins in a single vessel, providing a compact solution that enhances ion removal efficiency. Both systems serve various industries, including pharmaceuticals, electronics, and food processing, by delivering high-purity water essential for their operations.</p></p>
