<p><p>The food-grade rPET market consists primarily of two product types: pellets and flakes. rPET flakes are produced by washing and shredding post-consumer PET bottles, making them suitable for various applications, including food packaging. In contrast, rPET pellets are further processed from flakes, being melted and formed into uniform granules, ideal for injection molding or extrusion. Both forms allow manufacturers to create sustainable packaging solutions while maintaining food safety standards, thus supporting the circular economy for plastics.</p></p>
