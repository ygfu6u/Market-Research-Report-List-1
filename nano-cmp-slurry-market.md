<p><strong>The Nano CMP Slurry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colloidal Silica Slurries</li><li>Ceria Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nano CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) slurry market comprises primarily two types: colloidal silica slurries and ceria slurries. Colloidal silica slurries utilize silica particles suspended in a liquid medium, providing excellent planarization for silicon wafers and other semiconductor substrates. Ceria slurries, on the other hand, consist of cerium oxide particles that offer superior material removal rates and surface finish for demanding applications, particularly in the production of hard materials. Both types cater to the semiconductor and electronics industries for precision polishing.</p></p>
