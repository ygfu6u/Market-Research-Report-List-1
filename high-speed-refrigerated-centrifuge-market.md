Vertical high-speed refrigerated centrifuges, on the other hand, feature a vertical rotor design that maximizes sample capacity and performance, suitable for larger laboratories requiring extensive sample processing. Both types enhance precision in separation processes across various biomedical and research applications.</p></p>
