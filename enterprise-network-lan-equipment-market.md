<p><strong>The Enterprise Network LAN Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Token Ring</li><li>Fiber Distributed Data Interface (FDDI)</li><li>ARCNET</li><li>Ethernet</li><li>Wireless LAN (WLAN) or Wi-Fi</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Enterprise Network LAN Equipment market encompasses various technologies for local area networking. Token Ring operates on a token-passing protocol for data transmission. Fiber Distributed Data Interface (FDDI) utilizes fiber optics for high-speed network connections. ARCNET is an early standard focusing on token-passing over coaxial cables. Ethernet, the most prevalent, employs a carrier-sense multiple access method for efficient communication. Wireless LAN (WLAN), or Wi-Fi, allows device connectivity without cables, catering to the growing demand for mobility in enterprise environments.</p></p>
