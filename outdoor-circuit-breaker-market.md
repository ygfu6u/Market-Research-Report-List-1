<p><p>The outdoor circuit breaker market is categorized into three main types based on voltage levels. Low voltage circuit breakers operate below 1 kV, primarily for residential and commercial applications, ensuring safety in electrical systems. Medium voltage circuit breakers, ranging from 1 kV to 38 kV, are essential for industrial and utility applications, managing larger power distribution networks. High voltage circuit breakers, exceeding 38 kV, are critical for transmission systems, safeguarding infrastructure against overloads and fault conditions in large electrical grids.</p></p>
