<p><p>Iron deficiency anemia therapy market encompasses several treatment modalities. Oral iron therapy involves administering iron supplements in tablet form, suitable for most patients due to convenience and cost-effectiveness. Parenteral iron therapy delivers iron directly into the bloodstream, often used for those with absorption issues or severe anemia. Red blood cell transfusions provide immediate hemoglobin restoration in critical cases. Other therapies may include dietary adjustments or erythropoietin-stimulating agents aimed at boosting red blood cell production, addressing diverse patient needs.</p></p>
