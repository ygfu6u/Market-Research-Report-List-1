<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Center punch market types can be categorized into pneumatic and automatic systems. Pneumatic center punches utilize compressed air to deliver powerful impacts for creating indentations in materials, suitable for heavy-duty applications. They offer speed and efficiency, especially in industrial settings. On the other hand, automatic center punches function with a self-releasing mechanism, often powered by springs or electric motors, allowing for precise, consistent indentation without manual pressure. This makes them ideal for repetitive tasks in manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
