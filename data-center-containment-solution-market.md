<p><strong>The Data Center Containment Solution Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Aisle Containment</li><li>Hot Aisle Containment</li><li>Chimney Systems</li><li>Curtain Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data center containment solutions are designed to optimize cooling efficiency and reduce energy costs. **Cold aisle containment** restricts cool air to the area where servers draw in air, while **hot aisle containment** confines hot air exhaust from servers, preventing it from mixing with cooled air. **Chimney systems** utilize vertical structures to channel hot air away from equipment, while **curtain systems** use flexible barriers to segregate airflows. Other solutions may include innovative designs like blanking panels and room layouts tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
