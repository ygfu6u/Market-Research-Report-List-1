<p><p>The polybutene market primarily consists of two types: PB-1 and Polyisobutylene (PIB). PB-1 is often used in production applications such as adhesives, sealants, and coatings due to its high molecular weight and excellent adhesive properties. PIB, on the other hand, finds applications in lubricants, rubber modifiers, and as a component in fuel additives, given its superior water resistance and flexibility. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing product performance and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
