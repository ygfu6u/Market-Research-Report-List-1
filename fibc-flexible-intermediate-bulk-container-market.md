<p><p>The Flexible Intermediate Bulk Container (FIBC) market is categorized into four types based on their applications and safety features. Type A FIBCs are non-conductive and suitable for non-flammable materials. Type B FIBCs are designed to reduce the risk of ignition, suitable for flammable materials with a lower risk. Type C FIBCs are conductive and require grounding, making them ideal for handling flammable powders. Type D FIBCs are anti-static without grounding requirements, offering versatility in explosive environments while ensuring safety.</p></p>
