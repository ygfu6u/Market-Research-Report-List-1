<p><strong>The NdFeB Permanent Magnetic Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sintered NdFeB</li><li>Bonded NdFeB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NdFeB permanent magnetic materials market is primarily divided into two types: sintered NdFeB and bonded NdFeB. Sintered NdFeB is produced through a sintering process, resulting in high magnetic performance and moderate production costs, making it ideal for high-performance applications like motors and generators. In contrast, bonded NdFeB is created by mixing magnets with a polymer matrix, offering flexibility in shaping and lower production costs, suitable for applications requiring intricate designs and lower magnetic requirements.</p></p>
