<p><p>The System-on-Chip (SoC) market is categorized into three main types: digital, analog, and mixed-signal. Digital SoCs focus on processing digital signals and are commonly used in computing devices, providing efficient data handling and processing capabilities. Analog SoCs manage continuous signals, essential for applications like audio and radio frequency devices. Mixed-signal SoCs combine both digital and analog functionalities, allowing seamless interaction between the two types of signals, making them versatile for applications such as smartphones and IoT devices.</p></p>
