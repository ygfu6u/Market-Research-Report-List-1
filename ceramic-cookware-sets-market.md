<p><ul><li>Direct Fired Ceramics</li><li>Electric Stewed Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic cookware sets include various market types, primarily categorized into direct fired ceramics and electric stewed ceramics. Direct fired ceramics are designed for use over direct heat sources, such as gas or induction stoves, offering durability and even heat distribution for traditional cooking. Electric stewed ceramics, on the other hand, are specifically crafted for electric cooking appliances, providing convenience for slow-cooked meals and stews. Both types emphasize health benefits, non-toxic materials, and aesthetic appeal in kitchenware, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
