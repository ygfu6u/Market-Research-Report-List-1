<p><p>Vertical farming encompasses various innovative cultivation methods, primarily hydroponics, aeroponics, and other techniques. Hydroponics involves growing plants in nutrient-rich water solutions, eliminating soil and maximizing space efficiency. Aeroponics takes this further, using mist or aerosol to supply nutrients directly to the plant roots, resulting in faster growth and reduced water usage. Other methods include aquaponics, which combines fish farming with plant cultivation, and soil-based systems utilizing stacked layers. Each type enhances urban agriculture and addresses food security challenges.</p></p>
