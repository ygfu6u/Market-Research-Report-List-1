<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Packaging Aluminum Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Zero Aluminum Foil</li><li>Double Zero Aluminum Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical packaging aluminum foil market primarily includes two types: Single Zero Aluminum Foil and Double Zero Aluminum Foil. Single Zero Aluminum Foil features a thickness of 0.009mm to 0.018mm, providing effective barrier properties for moisture and light, making it suitable for drug packaging. Double Zero Aluminum Foil, on the other hand, is thinner, ranging from 0.007mm to 0.009mm, and is often used for applications requiring enhanced flexibility and precise sealing. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring product integrity and extending shelf life.</p></p>
