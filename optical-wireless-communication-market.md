<p><p>The optical wireless communication market encompasses three primary types: visible light communication (VLC), infrared communication, and ultraviolet communication. VLC utilizes LED lights to transmit data via visible light, offering high-speed connectivity, particularly in indoor settings. Infrared communication employs infrared light for short-range data transmission, commonly seen in remote controls and wireless networks. Ultraviolet communication, though less common, utilizes ultraviolet light for specialized applications, including secure data transmission and certain sensor technologies, catering to niche markets.</p></p>
