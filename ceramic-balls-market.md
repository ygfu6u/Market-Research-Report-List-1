<p><p>The ceramic balls market is categorized into inert and active ceramic balls. Inert ceramic balls are typically used as packing materials in various industrial applications, providing stability and structural support without chemically reacting with substances. In contrast, active ceramic balls are designed to engage with chemical processes, often featuring specific properties that enhance their reactivity and efficiency in catalysis or adsorption. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as petrochemicals, wastewater treatment, and air purification.</p></p>
