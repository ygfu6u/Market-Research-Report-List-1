<p><p>The Next Generation PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) equipment market is categorized into two main types: Two-Step and One-Step Next Generation PCR. Two-Step Next Generation PCR involves an initial reverse transcription step followed by PCR amplification, allowing for more efficient and precise amplification of RNA targets. In contrast, One-Step Next Generation PCR combines both RNA conversion and amplification in a single reaction, simplifying the process and reducing the risk of contamination, making it ideal for high-throughput applications.</p></p>
