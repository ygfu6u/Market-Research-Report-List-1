<p><ul><li>Coulometric Titration</li><li>Volumetric Titration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The KF titrator market is segmented into two primary types: coulometric titration and volumetric titration. Coulometric titration is used for analyzing low moisture content by generating titrant from the reaction of water with an electrolyte, providing high accuracy in micro-level measurements. Volumetric titration involves the direct addition of a standardized solution to the sample, suitable for measuring higher moisture levels. Both methods cater to various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals, each requiring specific moisture analysis techniques.</p></p>
