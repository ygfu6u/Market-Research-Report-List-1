<p><ul><li>Rigid Endoscopes</li><li>Flexible Endoscopes</li><li>Disposable Endoscopes</li><li>Capsule Endoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscope market comprises various types, each serving distinct purposes. Rigid endoscopes offer precision for procedures like arthroscopy and laparoscopy, providing clear images in stable environments. Flexible endoscopes are versatile for examining internal organs, such as the gastrointestinal tract, allowing maneuverability through anatomical curves. Disposable endoscopes prioritize hygiene and reduce infection risk, often used in short procedures. Capsule endoscopes are innovative, allowing patients to swallow a small camera to capture images of the digestive tract, enhancing non-invasive diagnostics.</p></p>
