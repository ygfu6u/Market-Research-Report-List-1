<p><p>The benzene-free carbomer market comprises three main types: Carbomer Homopolymer, Carbomer Copolymer, and Carbomer Interpolymer. Carbomer Homopolymer is a single polymer made from acrylic acid, offering thickening and stabilizing properties. Carbomer Copolymer combines two different types of polymers, enhancing compatibility and versatility in formulations. Carbomer Interpolymer consists of cross-linked structures that provide unique rheological behavior and improved performance in various applications. These types cater to diverse industries, including cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products, prioritizing safety and effectiveness.</p></p>
