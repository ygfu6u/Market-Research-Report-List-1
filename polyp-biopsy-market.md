<p><p>The polyp biopsy market encompasses various techniques for diagnosing polyps in different body systems. Colonoscopy-directed biopsies target the colon and rectum, while colposcopy-directed biopsies focus on the cervix to detect abnormalities. Esophagogastroduodenoscopy (EGD) targets the esophagus, stomach, and duodenum, enabling biopsy collection from these areas. Laryngoscopy allows for the examination and biopsy of the larynx.
