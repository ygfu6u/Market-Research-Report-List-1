<p><p>The electronic pest repellers market comprises two main types: electromagnetic and ultrasonic devices. Electromagnetic repellers generate low-frequency electromagnetic waves that disrupt pests' communication and navigation systems, effectively deterring them from invading spaces. Ultrasonic repellers emit high-frequency sound waves, inaudible to humans but irritating to pests, to discourage their presence. Both types are marketed as eco-friendly alternatives to chemical pesticides, appealing to consumers seeking safe and humane pest control solutions for homes and gardens.</p></p>
