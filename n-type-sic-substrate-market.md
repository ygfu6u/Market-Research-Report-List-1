<p><p>The N-type SiC substrate market features two main categories: semi-insulating and electric conductive. Semi-insulating SiC substrates exhibit high resistivity, making them ideal for high-frequency and high-power applications while minimizing electric losses. In contrast, electric conductive substrates have a lower resistivity, facilitating efficient current flow, which is crucial for power devices and RF applications. Both categories serve distinct roles in advanced electronic and optoelectronic devices, catering to the growing demand for efficient, high-performance materials in various industries.</p></p>
