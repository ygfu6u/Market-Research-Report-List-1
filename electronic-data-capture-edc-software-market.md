<p><p>Electronic Data Capture (EDC) software is classified into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise EDC solutions are installed directly on a user's hardware, providing full control over data security and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based EDC software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT overhead as users access the system via the internet. This model is often preferred for its ease of use and rapid deployment capabilities.</p></p>
