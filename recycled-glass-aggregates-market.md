<p><p>The recycled glass aggregates market primarily consists of two types: glass cullet and glass fines. Glass cullet refers to crushed, processed glass that is larger in size, commonly used in construction and road projects due to its durability and aesthetic appeal. Glass fines, on the other hand, are smaller particles resulting from further crushing, often utilized in applications like lightweight concrete or as a filler material. Both types contribute to sustainability by reducing landfill waste and promoting eco-friendly building practices.</p></p>
