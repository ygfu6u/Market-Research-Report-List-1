<p><p>The high-speed analog-to-digital converter (ADC) market is primarily segmented into single-channel and dual-channel types. Single-channel ADCs handle one signal input at a time, making them suitable for applications requiring high precision and speed in processing a single data stream. Dual-channel ADCs, conversely, can process two input signals simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and speed in data acquisition systems where multiple signals need to be captured concurrently. Both types are critical for applications in telecommunications, aerospace, automotive, and industrial automation.</p></p>
