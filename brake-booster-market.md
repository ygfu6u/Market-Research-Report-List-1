<p><strong>The Brake Booster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Booster</li><li>Hydraulic Booster</li><li>Air Pressure Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake booster market is categorized into three main types: vacuum boosters, hydraulic boosters, and air pressure boosters. Vacuum boosters use engine vacuum to amplify brake force, making them common in conventional vehicles. Hydraulic boosters generate braking power through hydraulic pressure, often found in advanced vehicles and trucks. Air pressure boosters utilize compressed air for enhanced braking efficiency, frequently used in heavy-duty and commercial vehicles. Each type offers different advantages based on vehicle design and performance requirements.</p></p>
