<p><p>The antenna coupler market includes several key types based on connector specifications. Standard TNC connectors offer robust performance for high-frequency applications, while Reverse Polarity TNC (RPTNC) connectors are designed to prevent mismatched connections, enhancing secure coupling in wireless communications. BNC connectors provide quick and reliable connections, making them ideal for video and RF applications. Meanwhile, Standard SMA connectors are compact and optimized for microwave frequencies, catering to a range of applications, including telecommunications and instrumentation. Each type serves unique needs within the market.</p></p>
