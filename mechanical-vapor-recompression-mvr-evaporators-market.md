<p><p>Mechanical Vapor Recompression (MVR) evaporators utilize energy-efficient methods to concentrate liquids by recycling vapor. The Compressed Steam System relies on mechanical compressors to elevate the pressure of steam, which is then used to heat feed liquid, enhancing evaporation efficiency. In contrast, the Water Vapor Distillation System utilizes low-grade heat sources and a vapor compression cycle to distill water, effectively purifying it while minimizing energy consumption. Both systems contribute to sustainable industrial processes and resource conservation.</p></p>
