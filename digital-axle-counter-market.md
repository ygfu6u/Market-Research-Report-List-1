<p><strong>The Digital Axle Counter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rail Side Axle Counter</li><li>On-rail Axle Counter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Axle Counter market comprises two primary types: Rail Side Axle Counters and On-rail Axle Counters. Rail Side Axle Counters are installed alongside the rail tracks, detecting the passing of trains by monitoring axle movement through sensor technologies. In contrast, On-rail Axle Counters are integrated directly onto the rolling stock, allowing real-time tracking of train movements and identification of axle numbers. Both types enhance safety and optimization in rail operations by ensuring accurate train counting and preventing collisions.</p></p>
