<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive ABS Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to generate voltage signals, providing accurate wheel speed data for anti-lock braking systems. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors rely on magnetic induction to detect wheel movement, offering a robust alternative for speed sensing. Both types are essential for optimizing vehicle safety and stability by preventing wheel lockup during braking, and they cater to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
