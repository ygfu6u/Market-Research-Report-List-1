<p><strong>The Flow Cell Sorter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent-activated Flow Cell Sorter</li><li>Magnetic-activated Flow Cell Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow cell sorter market is primarily categorized into two types: fluorescent-activated flow cell sorters and magnetic-activated flow cell sorters. Fluorescent-activated sorters utilize fluorescent tags on cells to sort them based on specific characteristics, enabling precise analysis of cellular properties. In contrast, magnetic-activated sorters employ magnetic beads to isolate target cells from a heterogeneous mixture, facilitating efficient separation based on cell surface markers. Both types are essential in research, clinical diagnostics, and biomanufacturing applications.</p></p>
