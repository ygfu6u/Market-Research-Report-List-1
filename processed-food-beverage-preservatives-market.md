Benzoates inhibit microbial growth, while nitrites are commonly used in meats to prevent spoilage and enhance color. Sulfites prevent oxidation in dried fruits and wines, whereas sorbates are effective against molds and yeast. Propionates are primarily used in baked goods to deter mold. Other preservatives encompass a range of synthetic and organic compounds.</p></p>
