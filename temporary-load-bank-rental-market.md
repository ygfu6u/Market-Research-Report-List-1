<p><ul><li>Resistive/Reactive Load Bank</li><li>Reactive Load Bank</li><li>Resistive Load Bank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temporary load bank rental market consists of resistive and reactive load banks, each serving distinct purposes. Resistive load banks simulate steady electrical loads, primarily testing generators and UPS systems by converting electrical energy into heat. Reactive load banks, on the other hand, provide variable loads to test power factor and generator performance under dynamic conditions. Both types are essential for ensuring reliable operation in sectors like telecommunications, data centers, and emergency power applications, enhancing equipment efficiency and performance.</p></p>
