<p><p>The vehicle safety device market is broadly classified into active and passive safety systems. Active safety systems are designed to prevent accidents by using technologies like anti-lock braking systems, electronic stability control, and adaptive cruise control. These systems take proactive measures to enhance vehicle stability and control. In contrast, passive safety systems focus on minimizing injury during an accident through features such as airbags, seatbelts, and crumple zones. Both systems are essential for improving overall vehicle safety and reducing fatalities on the road.</p></p>
