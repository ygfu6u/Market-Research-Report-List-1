<p><p>The Bluetooth communication chip market is divided into Single Mode and Dual Mode segments. Single Mode chips only support Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) functionality, making them ideal for applications requiring low power consumption, such as wearables and IoT devices. Dual Mode chips, on the other hand, support both Classic Bluetooth and BLE, offering versatility for devices that need to connect with a wider range of Bluetooth-enabled products, such as audio devices, smartphones, and various peripherals.</p></p>
