<p><p>The diagnostic/medical imaging market encompasses various technologies used to visualize internal body structures for diagnosis and treatment. X-ray imaging systems utilize radiation to capture images of bones and tissues. Computed tomography (CT) scanners provide cross-sectional views for detailed examination. Ultrasound systems use sound waves for real-time imaging, commonly in obstetrics. Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) systems employ strong magnetic fields and radio waves for soft tissue imaging. Nuclear imaging systems utilize radioactive substances to assess organ function and detect abnormalities.</p></p>
