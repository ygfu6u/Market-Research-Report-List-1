<p><p>Mining tailings management encompasses various methods for storing and disposing of waste materials from mineral extraction. Dry stacking involves the dewatering of tailings, allowing them to be stacked in a solid form, minimizing environmental risks. Underground storage entails depositing tailings in mined-out areas, reducing surface impact but requiring careful monitoring. Other management methods may include traditional tailings dams and innovative techniques like paste backfill or geotextile filtration. Each method aims to ensure safety, environmental protection, and resource efficiency.</p></p>
