<p><p>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) is a vacuum-based coating process used to deposit thin films on various substrates. This technique involves the physical transition of material from a condensed phase to a vapor phase, followed by the deposition of this vapor onto a surface, forming a thin layer. PVD is widely used in industries such as electronics, optics, and automotive for applications including semiconductor fabrication, decorative coatings, and tool manufacturing.</p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for advanced materials and enhanced surface properties across various industries.
