<p><p>DC-DC converters are essential for electric vehicles (EVs), converting one DC voltage level to another for various applications. In the isolated market, the converter uses a transformer to separate input and output circuits, enhancing safety and allowing for higher voltage conversion. Non-isolated converters, on the other hand, do not use transformers, providing more compact, lighter designs suitable for lower voltage applications. Both types play crucial roles in managing power for EV batteries, motors, and ancillary systems.</p></p>
