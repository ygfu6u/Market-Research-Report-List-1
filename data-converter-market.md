<p><p>The data converter market primarily consists of two types: Analog-to-Digital Converters (ADCs) and Digital-to-Analog Converters (DACs). ADCs convert analog signals, such as sound or temperature, into digital data for processing and analysis in electronic systems. Conversely, DACs transform digital data back into analog signals, enabling devices to produce audible sound or visual output. Both types are essential in various applications, including telecommunications, consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial automation, driving innovation and enhancing data processing capabilities.</p></p>
