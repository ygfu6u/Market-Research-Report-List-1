<p><p>The automotive OE lighting market includes Halogen, HID Xenon, and LED lights. Halogen lights are cost-effective and widely used, offering decent brightness but lower efficiency. HID Xenon lights provide brighter, whiter illumination with higher energy efficiency compared to halogen but are more expensive. LED lights are the most advanced, offering superior brightness, energy efficiency, and longer lifespan. They are increasingly popular in modern vehicles due to their compact design and versatility, though they come at a higher cost. Each type caters to different performance and budget needs.</p></p>
