<p><p>The metallic couplings market primarily includes chain couplings and gear couplings, each serving distinct purposes in transmitting power between shafts. Chain couplings, using chains and sprockets, are ideal for high-torque applications and accommodate misalignments. They are robust and suitable for heavy-duty machinery. Gear couplings consist of two gears that mesh, providing a compact solution for torque transmission with minimal space requirements. They excel in precise alignment and high-speed operations, making them versatile in industrial applications.</p></p>
