<p><strong>The Double Break Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bus Side</li><li>Line Side</li><li>Transfer Bus Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Double Break Isolator Market is categorized into three key types: Bus Side, Line Side, and Transfer Bus Side. Bus Side isolators are positioned between the busbar and the outgoing feeder, ensuring safety during maintenance. Line Side isolators are located between the power source and the busbar, protecting the system from faults. Transfer Bus Side isolators facilitate seamless switching between two bus systems, enhancing reliability and operational flexibility. Together, these types serve critical roles in electrical distribution systems.</p></p>
