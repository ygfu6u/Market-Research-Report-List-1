<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared proximity sensor market is divided into two main types: active and passive sensors. Active infrared sensors emit infrared light and detect reflections from nearby objects, enabling precise distance measurement and obstacle detection. These are commonly used in automation and safety applications. Conversely, passive infrared sensors detect ambient infrared radiation emitted by objects without emitting any light themselves, making them energy-efficient and ideal for motion detection in security systems and energy-saving applications.</p></p>
