<p><p>The epoxy resin mold release tape market is divided into two main types: single-sided and double-sided tapes. Single-sided tapes feature adhesive on one side, allowing for easy application to molds, ensuring smooth demolding of cured resins. Double-sided tapes, on the other hand, offer adhesive on both sides, providing a stronger bond between substrates and molds, ideal for complex or intricate shapes. Both types are essential in different mold-making applications, enhancing efficiency and improving the quality of finished products.</p></p>
