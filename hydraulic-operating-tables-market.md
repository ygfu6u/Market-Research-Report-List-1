<p><ul><li>Electro-Hydraulic Operating Table</li><li>Manual-Hydraulic Operating Table</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic operating tables market is divided into two primary types: electro-hydraulic and manual-hydraulic operating tables. Electro-hydraulic tables utilize electric power to adjust positioning and height, enhancing precision and ease of use in surgical settings. Manual-hydraulic tables require mechanical effort to adjust, making them typically more cost-effective but less convenient. Both types cater to varying hospital needs, with electro-hydraulic models favored for complex surgeries requiring rapid and precise positioning, while manual options serve smaller facilities with budget constraints.</p></p>
