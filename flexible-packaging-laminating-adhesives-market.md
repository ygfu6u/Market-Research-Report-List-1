<p><strong>The Flexible Packaging Laminating Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Solvent Less</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible packaging laminating adhesives market comprises three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and solvent-less adhesives. Water-based adhesives use water as a solvent, offering low VOC emissions and a safer option for food packaging. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong adhesion but may have higher environmental impacts. Solvent-less adhesives are used for their high efficiency and minimal environmental footprint, as they contain no solvents, making them ideal for applications that require fast curing and durability in flexible packaging.</p></p>
