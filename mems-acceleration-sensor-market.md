<p><p>The MEMS acceleration sensor market is categorized mainly into capacitive and thermal sensors. Capacitive MEMS acceleration sensors operate by measuring changes in capacitance resulting from acceleration-induced movement, offering high sensitivity and low power consumption, making them suitable for consumer electronics and automotive applications. In contrast, thermal MEMS acceleration sensors detect changes in temperature distribution caused by acceleration, providing robust performance in harsh environments. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics, each with unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
