<p><strong>The Grounding Busbar Trunking Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Segregated Bus Duct</li><li>Nonsegregated Bus Duct</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grounding busbar trunking systems consist of Segregated and Nonsegregated bus ducts, each serving specific applications. Segregated bus ducts feature a distinct compartmentalization for each phase, enhancing safety and reducing the risk of short circuits. This type allows efficient heat dissipation and minimizes electromagnetic interference. In contrast, Nonsegregated bus ducts combine all phases in a single enclosure, offering a more compact design. While they can be economical, they may face challenges in heat management and electromagnetic interference control compared to segregated systems.</p></p>
