<p><p>Reactive splitters are components in telecommunications that manage signal distribution. The 2-way reactive splitter divides a single input signal into two output signals, ensuring minimal loss and maintaining signal integrity. A 3-way reactive splitter offers one input and three outputs, catering to more complex distribution needs. The 4-way reactive splitter serves a similar function, distributing the signal into four outputs. Each type enhances flexibility in network design while optimizing performance across various applications, including broadcasting and data communication.</p></p>
