<p><ul><li>Dynamic Headphones</li><li>Moving Iron Headphones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The professional headphone market primarily includes dynamic and moving iron headphones. Dynamic headphones utilize a diaphragm and magnet to produce sound, offering a rich bass and wide frequency response, making them suitable for studio monitoring and casual listening. On the other hand, moving iron headphones use tiny armatures to generate sound, resulting in a more detailed audio reproduction and compact design. These are often favored by audiophiles and musicians for critical listening and precision in sound performance.</p></p>
