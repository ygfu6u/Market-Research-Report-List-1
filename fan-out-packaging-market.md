<p><p>The Fan-Out Packaging market is classified into two main types: Core Fan-Out Packaging and High-Density Fan-Out Packaging. Core Fan-Out Packaging focuses on integrating multiple chips into a single package, enhancing performance and reducing size, making it ideal for applications like smartphones. In contrast, High-Density Fan-Out Packaging offers greater interconnection density and improved thermal management, catering to advanced applications such as high-performance computing and AI. Both types aim to optimize space and efficiency in semiconductor integration.</p></p>
