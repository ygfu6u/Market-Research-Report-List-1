<p><p>The smartphone wireless game controllers market consists of two main types: bracket-mounted and clip-mounted controllers. Bracket-mounted controllers attach directly to the smartphone, providing a stable grip and improved ergonomics for gameplay. They are often designed to accommodate various smartphone sizes. In contrast, clip-mounted controllers feature a clip that secures the smartphone at a distance from the controller, allowing for a more versatile setup and easier access to the touchscreen. Both types enhance the gaming experience by offering better control and comfort.</p></p>
