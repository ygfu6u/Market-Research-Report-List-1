<p><p>The portable colorimeter market is segmented based on color measurement systems. CIE LAB offers a model based on human vision perception, useful for various industries. The XYZ system provides device-independent color representation, while the LCh model focuses on chromaticity and lightness. The RGB model is based on primary colors, widely used in digital applications. Lastly, the LUV model incorporates luminance and chromaticity, enhancing color differentiation. Each type serves specific applications across industries like textiles, paints, and food.</p></p>
