<p><p>The Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) adhesives market is divided into two primary types based on viscosity: low viscosity and high viscosity. Low viscosity PVA adhesives are fluid and easily spreadable, making them ideal for applications requiring quick bonding and ease of use, such as paper and crafts. On the other hand, high viscosity PVA adhesives are thicker, providing stronger bonds for heavier materials like wood and textiles, often used in construction and manufacturing sectors. Each type serves distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
