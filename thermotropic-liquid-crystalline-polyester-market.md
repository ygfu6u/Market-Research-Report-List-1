<p><p>The thermotropic liquid crystalline polyester market comprises various processing methods, primarily injection molding and extrusion molding, along with other specialized techniques. Injection molding involves shaping the polyester into intricate designs by injecting melted material into molds, ideal for high-volume production. Extrusion molding, on the other hand, involves forcing the polyester through a die to create continuous shapes or profiles, suitable for applications like films and sheets. Other methods include blow molding and compression molding, catering to diverse applications and designs.</p></p>
