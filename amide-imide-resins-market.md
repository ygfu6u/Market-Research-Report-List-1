<p><p>Amide-imide resins are high-performance thermosetting polymers used in various applications, including electronics and aerospace. The market is categorized into two main types: linear amide-imide resins and branched amide-imide resins. Linear amide-imide resins exhibit a simple, straight-chain structure, offering excellent thermal stability and mechanical properties, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, branched amide-imide resins feature a more complex, branched structure, enhancing flexibility and impact resistance, ideal for specialized applications requiring superior toughness and durability.</p></p>
