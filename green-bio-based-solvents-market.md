Esters are derived from fatty acids and offer low toxicity and biodegradability, making them ideal for industrial applications. Alcohols, such as ethanol and propanol, provide excellent solvency and are often used in formulations. D-limonene, extracted from citrus fruits, serves as a natural solvent with degreasing properties. Other solvents may include plant-based terpenes and oils, enhancing sustainability in various sectors like coatings, adhesives, and cleaning products.</p></p>
