<p><strong>The Low Voltage Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Voltage Relay</li><li>Static Voltage Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low voltage relay market is primarily categorized into two types: electromagnetic voltage relays and static voltage relays. Electromagnetic voltage relays use mechanical components and magnetic fields to function, providing reliable switching in various applications. In contrast, static voltage relays rely on semiconductor technology, offering faster response times and greater precision with no moving parts. Both types serve critical roles in controlling electrical systems, protecting equipment from voltage fluctuations, and ensuring efficient operation in various industries.</p></p>
