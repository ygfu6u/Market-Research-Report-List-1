<p><p>In the modular servers market, three key types are prominent: Reduced Instruction Set Computing (RISC), System-On-Chip (SoC), and x86 architectures. RISC focuses on simplicity and efficiency, enabling high performance with lower power consumption, ideal for specific applications. SoC integrates multiple components onto a single chip, enhancing compactness and energy efficiency, making it perfect for mobile and embedded systems. The x86 market dominates with its compatibility and performance for a wide range of server applications, catering to diverse processing needs.</p></p>
