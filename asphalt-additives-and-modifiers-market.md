<p><p>The asphalt additives and modifiers market encompasses two primary types: asphalt additives and asphalt modifiers. Asphalt additives are substances incorporated into asphalt mixtures to enhance specific properties, such as adhesion, durability, and performance under varying temperatures. On the other hand, asphalt modifiers are materials that alter the physical and chemical characteristics of asphalt, improving its resistance to aging, cracking, and deformation. Together, these components aim to optimize asphalt performance for various applications, including roads, pavements, and roofing.</p></p>
