<p><p>Retarders are devices used in mechanical engineering to enhance braking performance by providing additional resistance. The market for retarders is primarily divided into two types: electric retarders and hydraulic retarders. Electric retarders utilize electrical energy to generate braking torque, offering precise control and low maintenance. Hydraulic retarders, on the other hand, use fluid dynamics to create resistance, providing robust braking capabilities, especially in heavy vehicles. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing safety and performance in various transportation applications.</p></p>
