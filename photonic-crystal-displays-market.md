<p><p>The photonic crystal display market is segmented into three main types based on dimensionality. Three-dimensional photonic crystals offer unique light manipulation capabilities, enhancing color and brightness for advanced display technologies. Two-dimensional photonic crystals are primarily used in applications requiring flat surfaces, providing superior optical properties and efficient light control. One-dimensional photonic crystals are simpler structures, often employed in basic display applications and filters, optimizing light transmission and reflection. Each type has unique advantages suited to specific technological demands in display applications.</p></p>
