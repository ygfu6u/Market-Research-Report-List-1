<p><strong>The Fiberglass Mat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chopped Strand Mat</li><li>Air Laid Mat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass mat market is primarily divided into two types: Chopped Strand Mat and Air Laid Mat. Chopped Strand Mat consists of short fiberglass strands randomly oriented and bonded with a resin, offering versatility and strength for various applications, such as automotive and construction. In contrast, Air Laid Mat involves a different production process where dry fiberglass strands are laid onto a surface using air, creating a lightweight, porous structure ideal for applications requiring good wet-out and resin absorption, such as in composite materials.</p></p>
