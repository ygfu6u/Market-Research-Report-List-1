<p><strong>The Volumetric Display Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static-volume Display</li><li>Swept-volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Volumetric Display Devices can be categorized into two main types: static-volume displays and swept-volume displays. Static-volume displays create three-dimensional images that maintain their form and position within defined space, often utilizing technologies like holography or particle manipulation. In contrast, swept-volume displays generate 3D visuals by moving an illuminating source through space, effectively 'sweeping' out a volume to form images, often relying on techniques like rotating screens or laser projections. Both market types enhance visual representation and immersive experiences across various applications.</p></p>
