<p><p>The window regulator market is divided into two main types: electric and manual window regulators. Electric window regulators facilitate the smooth operation of car windows with the push of a button, often featuring advanced technologies like anti-pinch mechanisms for safety. They are commonly found in modern vehicles, offering convenience and enhanced functionality. In contrast, manual window regulators require physical effort to operate, using a mechanical cranking system. Though less common in newer models, they are still favored in budget-friendly vehicles for simplicity and reliability.</p></p>
