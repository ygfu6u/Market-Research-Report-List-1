<p><p>The PMMA IOL market encompasses several types of lenses designed for improved vision post-cataract surgery. Multifocal intraocular lenses provide multiple focal points, enabling clear vision at various distances. Adjustable intraocular lenses allow patients to modify their vision preferences post-surgery through external means. Aspheric intraocular lenses are designed with a specific curvature that reduces spherical aberrations, enhancing image quality and contrast sensitivity. Together, these innovations cater to diverse patient needs, enhancing outcomes and overall satisfaction in vision correction.</p></p>
