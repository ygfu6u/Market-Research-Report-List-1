<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water/Aqueous-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial part washer market is primarily divided into two types: solvent-based and water/aqueous-based systems. Solvent-based washers utilize organic solvents to dissolve oils and greases, offering rapid drying and effective cleaning for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, water/aqueous-based systems use water mixed with detergents or surfactants, focusing on environmental friendliness and safety. These systems are preferred for less extreme cleaning needs and are often more cost-effective. Both types serve essential roles in maintaining equipment and ensuring product quality in various industries.</p></p>
