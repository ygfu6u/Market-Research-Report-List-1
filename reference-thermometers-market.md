<p><p>The reference thermometers market encompasses three main types: Platinum Resistance Thermometers (PRTs), thermocouples, and precision thermistors. PRTs offer high accuracy and stability, making them ideal for laboratory and industrial applications. Thermocouples, known for their wide temperature range and ruggedness, are widely used in various industries but may have lower precision. Precision thermistors provide high sensitivity and quick response times, suitable for applications requiring precise temperature measurements. Together, these devices cater to diverse sectors, ensuring accurate temperature control and monitoring.</p></p>
