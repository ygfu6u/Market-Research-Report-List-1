<p><p>The commercial digital door lock market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Magnetic Stripe Locks** utilize magnetic cards to grant access, popular for ease of management and low cost.</p><p>2. **Electromechanical Door Locks** combine electronic components with mechanical locking, offering enhanced security and versatility, ideal for high-traffic areas.</p><p>3. **Electric Strike Locks** function with an electronic strike plate that releases the lock upon receiving an electric signal, allowing integration with various access control systems. Together, these locks cater to diverse security needs in commercial settings.</p></p>
