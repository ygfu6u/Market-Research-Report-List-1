<p><p>The Multi Corrugated Wine Box market comprises three primary types based on board thickness: Single Wall Board, Double Wall Board, and Triple Wall Board. Single Wall Boxes, made from one layer of corrugated board, are lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for short-distance transport. Double Wall Boxes offer enhanced protection with two layers, ideal for longer shipping or premium wines. Triple Wall Boxes provide maximum durability with three layers, designed for high-value or fragile wines requiring substantial cushioning during transit.</p></p>
