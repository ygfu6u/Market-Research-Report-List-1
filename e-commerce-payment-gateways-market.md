<p><p>E-commerce payment gateways can be categorized into online and offline modes. </p><p>Online mode enables real-time transactions through the internet, facilitating instant payments via credit cards, digital wallets, or bank transfers while providing security through encryption and fraud detection. This mode is prevalent in e-commerce platforms.</p><p>Offline mode, on the other hand, involves transactions that occur without immediate internet access, such as processing payments via phone orders or in-person transactions using card readers. This mode often serves traditional retail or mobile sales contexts.</p></p>
