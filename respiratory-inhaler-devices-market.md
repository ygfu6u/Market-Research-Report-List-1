<p><p>The respiratory inhaler devices market includes three primary types: Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs), Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs), and Nebulizers. DPIs deliver medication in powdered form, requiring patients to inhale deeply to activate the dose. MDIs, commonly recognized, use a propellant to spray medication in aerosol form for inhalation. Nebulizers transform liquid medication into a fine mist, making it easier for patients, especially young children or severe respiratory cases, to inhale the treatment over a longer duration.</p></p>
