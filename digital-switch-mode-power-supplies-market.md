<p><p>The digital switch-mode power supply (SMPS) market is primarily segmented into AC/DC and DC/DC types. AC/DC converters transform alternating current from the electrical grid into direct current for powering electronic devices. They are widely used in applications like chargers and power adapters. Conversely, DC/DC converters manage voltage levels within DC systems, ensuring efficient energy transfer between different voltage domains, often utilized in battery-powered devices and renewable energy systems. Both market types emphasize efficiency and compact designs for diverse applications.</p></p>
