<p><p>The sand washing and recycling machine market includes primarily two types: wheel sand washing machines and screw sand washing machines. Wheel sand washing machines utilize a circular wheel to clean sand, effectively removing impurities and ensuring a finer product. In contrast, screw sand washing machines employ a spiral design to lift sand and separate impurities, making them suitable for high-density materials. Both types are essential in construction, mining, and aggregate industries for producing high-quality sand while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
