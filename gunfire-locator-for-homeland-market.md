<p><p>Gunfire locators for the homeland market come in two main types: acoustic and optical systems. Acoustic locators utilize advanced microphones to detect and triangulate the sound of gunfire, providing real-time location data to law enforcement and security agencies. In contrast, optical systems employ cameras and image processing technology to visually identify and track the source of gunfire through visual cues, such as muzzle flashes. Both technologies enhance situational awareness and response capabilities in urban environments, improving public safety.</p></p>
