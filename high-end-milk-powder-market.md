<p><p>The high-end milk powder market encompasses two primary types: whole milk powder and skim milk powder. Whole milk powder retains the fat content of fresh milk, providing rich flavor and creaminess, making it popular in premium products like indulgent chocolates and desserts. In contrast, skim milk powder has had the fat removed, offering a lower-calorie option with concentrated nutrition, often used in health-focused beverages and sports nutrition products. Both cater to diverse consumer preferences for quality and health.</p></p>
