The demand for Wi-Fi extenders has surged due to the growing reliance on wireless internet for activities such as streaming, gaming, and remote work.</p><p>The Wi-Fi Extender Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Wi-Fi extender market comprises various types, primarily focusing on Wi-Fi range extenders and wireless access point (AP) routers. Wi-Fi range extenders amplify existing signals, enhancing coverage in larger spaces or dead zones. Conversely, wireless AP routers create new access points to broaden network connectivity, often connecting via Ethernet to improve speeds and reliability. Both options aim to optimize Wi-Fi performance, catering to diverse user needs like increased coverage, reduced latency, and improved streaming experiences in homes and businesses.</p></p>
