<p><ul><li>Carbon-Composition Resistor</li><li>Carbon-Film Resistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon resistor market primarily includes two types: carbon-composition resistors and carbon-film resistors. Carbon-composition resistors, made from a mixture of carbon and binding materials, offer high stability and can handle high energy pulses, making them suitable for audio equipment. In contrast, carbon-film resistors are produced by depositing a thin layer of carbon film on a ceramic substrate, providing better tolerance and lower noise. Both types serve diverse electronic applications, with varying characteristics suited for specific requirements in circuit design.</p></p>
