<p><ul><li>Direct Electrolytic Oxidation</li><li>Indirect Electrolytic Oxidation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electro-oxidation system market is segmented into two main types: direct electrolysis and indirect electrolysis. Direct electrolytic oxidation involves the application of an electric current to the waste water, facilitating the removal of pollutants through direct oxidation at the anode. In contrast, indirect electrolytic oxidation relies on generating reactive species, such as hydroxyl radicals, that effectively oxidize contaminants in a more versatile manner. Both methods are integral for treating wastewater and enhancing environmental sustainability.</p></p>
