<p><p>The ablation catheters market is segmented into two main types: multielectrode ablation catheters and single-point ablation catheters. Multielectrode ablation catheters are equipped with multiple electrodes that allow for simultaneous energy delivery across a larger surface area, enhancing efficiency during procedures. In contrast, single-point ablation catheters focus energy on a specific point, offering precision in targeted therapies. Both types play crucial roles in treating various cardiac and non-cardiac conditions, catering to differing procedural needs and patient conditions.</p></p>
