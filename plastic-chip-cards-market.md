<p><ul><li>Contact Cards</li><li>Contactless Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic chip cards market comprises two primary types: contact cards and contactless cards. Contact cards require physical insertion into a reader, establishing a direct electrical connection for secure transactions, commonly used in banking and identity verification. In contrast, contactless cards utilize radio frequency identification (RFID) technology, allowing users to make transactions by simply waving the card near a compatible reader, enhancing convenience and speed. Both types play a crucial role in the growing demand for secure payment solutions globally.</p></p>
