<p><strong>The GigE Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GigE camera market is segmented primarily into line scan and area scan cameras, each catering to specific imaging needs. Line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for high-speed applications and continuous moving objects, such as conveyor systems. In contrast, area scan cameras capture entire images in a single shot, suitable for static scenes and applications requiring detailed analysis, like quality control in manufacturing. Both types leverage GigE technology for high-speed data transmission.</p></p>
