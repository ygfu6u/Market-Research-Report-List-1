<p><p>The Automotive 3D Light Detection and Ranging (LiDAR) market comprises two main types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR utilizes fixed components with no moving parts, offering durability and compact design, suitable for high-volume production in vehicles. In contrast, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR employs rotating components to capture a 360-degree view, providing high-resolution data over large areas. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing autonomous driving technologies and enhancing vehicle safety through precise environmental mapping.</p></p>
