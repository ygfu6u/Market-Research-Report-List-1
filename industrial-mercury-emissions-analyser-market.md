<p><strong>The Industrial Mercury Emissions Analyser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Mercury Emissions Analyzer market features two primary types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) Mercury Analyzers and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF) Mercury Analyzers. CVAA utilizes cold vapor techniques to measure mercury concentration by absorbing light at specific wavelengths, providing high sensitivity and reliability for emissions monitoring. In contrast, CAF analyzers detect mercury by measuring fluorescence emitted when mercury is exposed to ultraviolet light, offering improved detection limits and faster response times. Both technologies are essential for regulatory compliance and environmental protection.</p></p>
