<p><p>The carbohydrases market comprises various enzyme types, each targeting specific polysaccharides. Cellulases break down cellulose into glucose, aiding in biofuel production and paper processing. Amylases convert starch into sugars, essential for baking and brewing industries. Mannanases hydrolyze mannan, enhancing fiber digestion in animal feed. Pectinases degrade pectin, improving juice extraction and clarity. Lactase is crucial for lactose digestion, benefiting dairy industries and lactose-intolerant consumers. Together, these enzymes play vital roles in food processing, animal nutrition, and biotechnology.</p></p>
