<p><p>The brakes market consists of various components essential for vehicle safety. Brake pads are crucial for disc brakes, providing friction to slow or stop the vehicle. Brake rotors are metal discs that work with brake pads, dissipating heat. Brake drums encase the brake shoes in drum brake systems, offering reliable stopping power. Together, these components ensure effective braking systems.</p></p>
