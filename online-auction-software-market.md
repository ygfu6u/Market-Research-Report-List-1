<p><p>Online auction software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based auction software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access the platform via the internet, promoting flexibility and scalability. This option reduces maintenance costs and offers automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises auction software is installed locally on a user's hardware, providing greater control and customization but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Both types cater to different user needs based on operational preferences and resources.</p></p>
