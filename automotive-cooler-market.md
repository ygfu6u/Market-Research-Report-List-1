<p><ul><li>Plate and Fin</li><li>Tube and Fin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive cooler market primarily consists of two types: plate and fin coolers and tube and fin coolers. Plate and fin coolers feature multiple thin plates stacked together, enhancing thermal efficiency and compact design, making them suitable for tight spaces. Tube and fin coolers consist of a series of tubes surrounded by fins, allowing for effective heat dissipation over larger areas. Both types cater to the needs of modern vehicles, ensuring optimal engine performance and temperature regulation.</p></p>
