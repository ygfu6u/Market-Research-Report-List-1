<p><p>The agricultural pheromones market can be categorized into three main types: sex pheromones, aggregation pheromones, and others. Sex pheromones attract mating partners, effectively managing pest populations by disrupting their reproduction. Aggregation pheromones, on the other hand, lure pests to specific areas, facilitating monitoring and trapping. The "others" category includes alarm, trail-following, and host-location pheromones, which serve various agricultural purposes, enhancing crop protection and productivity by targeting specific pest behaviors and communication.</p></p>
