<p><p>The urinary protein reagent market comprises three primary types: ELISA-based reagents, colorimetric method-based reagents, and others. ELISA-based reagents are utilized for their high sensitivity and specificity in detecting protein levels in urine samples. Colorimetric method-based reagents offer a simpler, cost-effective approach, using color changes to indicate protein concentration. Other reagents may include electrophoresis and immunological techniques, catering to diverse laboratory needs. Together, these types facilitate effective urinary protein analysis for diagnostic and monitoring purposes in clinical settings.</p></p>
